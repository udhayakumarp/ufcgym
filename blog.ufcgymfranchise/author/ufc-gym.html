Featured Article: Michael Bisping Named 2016 FOX Sports™ Fighter of the Year

Fan-favorite and UFC Middleweight Champion, Michael Bisping, has been named FOX Sports™ Fighter of the Year for 2016. Bisping has been busy this year with 3 consecutive wins against the likes of legends Dan Henderson and Anderson Silva. Along with the announcement of opening his very own UFC GYM in Costa Mesa alongside UFC fighter Cub Swanson, it's safe to say Bisping has had a very good year.
