UFC GYM Vietnam is First in Asia

Posted By: Francis Yango
Posted On: Nov 11, 2016

UFC GYM announced its first, official expansion into Asia with a $15 million-dollar investment led by its new partners in the region, CMG.ASIA.  Tuesday, October 18th, UFC GYM Vietnam held its Grand Opening celebration. The gym is located at Udic Tower in Cau Giay District Hanoi and serves as the first of several gyms to open in this region.


“We are thrilled that UFC Gym is expanding into Southeast Asia," said Kenneth Berger, UFC Executive Vice President and General Manager - Asia, “Asia is one of UFC’s fastest growing markets and we want to bring signature MMA styles of training and fitness to all fight fans in this region. Our fans and fitness consumers in Southeast Asia understand UFC’s brand and we can’t wait for them to train at our signature gyms.”

Watch the video to check out their awesome, celebrity-filled opening event!


UFC GYM continues it's rapid growth, having opened 50 clubs in the past two years and another 50 expected within the next year. This expansion has led to more than 135 locations globally.
 
Having created its training programs through private access to the training regimens of some of the most lauded UFC athletes, UFC GYM allows members of all ages and fitness levels to work out like UFC athletes and truly Train Different.
