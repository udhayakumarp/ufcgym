The MATRX class is uniquely structured to combine TRX suspension bodyweight exercises and our UFC GYM MMA cardio conditioning in order to build endurance, strength, balance, flexibility and core stability simultaneously. A basic class includes cardio calisthenics, suspension training, shadow kickboxing, sprawls, technical standing, break falling, pummeling, sitting through, bag work (standing and ground), and core strengthening exercises. The 150 lb. heavy bags and TRX suspension trainer are the primary focus of the class.

UFC FIT
Class Description

UFC FIT class offers a total body workout that follows a set series of drills completed in six five-minute rounds accompanied by a warm-up and cool down. The drills are structured to challenge cardio capacity, build strength, and improve balance and flexibility. UFC FIT class offers the perfect full-body high-intensity interval training (HIIT). UFC FIT class is great for cross training and provides a unique mix of functional training and cardio that challenges the entire body in true MMA style.
